Google

Last summer in 2019 I attended the Google Computer Science Summer Institute, CSSI for short, in the Cambridge MA office. We lived in MIT dorms and walked to the office every day for a number of weeks. This was my first real exposure to any actual programming, although I've always had an interest in math. After taking my first CS course at Dartmouth, I decided to apply for thge Google STEP internship this summer. Sadly, I'm not working in the Sunnyvale office where I was assigned but I'm lucky enough to be participating with a team from the NYC office! I look forward to developing my CS skills and meeting new Googlers!

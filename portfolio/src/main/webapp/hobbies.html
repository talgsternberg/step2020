My Hobbies

Surfing: Despite the fact I live about 45 minutes away from any surfing spots, I still like to surf every weekend. My favorite spots are Nantasket Beach, Rye Rocks, and the Wall. I've surfed along the California Coast and in the Dominican Republic!

Hiking: I like to spend a lot of time outside. Luckily, I go to school in the mountains and I'm able to get out a lot. In the winter, I also like to go skiing!

If you're a surfer too, check out Magic Seaweed! It is a website/app that allows you to access a ton of information about different surf spots around the world. The information ranges from current surf conditions to local tips and warnings
